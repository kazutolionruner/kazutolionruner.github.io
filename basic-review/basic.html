Why I like to go swimming in the summer.

Swimming is my most favorite activity in the summer. When the sun is shinning and the air is warm, you will find me dipping into my backyard pool. It's not an impressive pool, only three feet deep, but its mine.

There are three reasons I like to swim:

  • I get lots of expersize
  • I enjoy the freedom
  • I have the opportunity to be in the sun
